# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in TerraHerb respectful, harassment-free, and inclusive for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, personal attacks, or public/private intimidation
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove or edit comments, commits, issues, or PRs that violate these standards.

## Scope
This code of conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and related community channels.

## Reporting
To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through a private issue or repository contact channel. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.
